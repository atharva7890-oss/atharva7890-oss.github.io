Famous Dishes

Jowar Bhakri

Jowar Bhakri is a traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour.

Shenga Chutney

This is a dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices.

Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian street food made with spicy sprouted lentil curry.

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils.
